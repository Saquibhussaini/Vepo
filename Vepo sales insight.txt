As a Data Analytics Dashboard is our face, today I analyzed one-year sales data from online stores. Initially, I collected real-world data and then cleaned and processed it using Sample Queries. In this process, I developed the necessary logic using other data and created various charts. To facilitate the review of different insights, I incorporated filters. Through this dashboard, I uncovered valuable insights and arrived at a conclusion, suggesting an effective improvement to boost sales for this online shop.

Sample Queries:-
1. Compare the sales & orders using a single chart.
2. Which Month got the height & lowest sales & order?
3. Who purchased more- Men or Women in 2022?
4. What is the different order status in 2022
5. List of Top 5 states contributing to the sales.
6. Relation between age & gender based on number of orders.
7. Which channel is contributing to maximum sales?
8. Highest selling categories.

Sample insights:- 
1. Women have a higher likelihood of making purchases compared to men (65%).
2. Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh are the three leading states (35%).
3. The age group of adults between 30 and 49 years old makes the maximum contribution (50%).
4. Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra are the primary channels with the highest contribution (80%).
5. Sizes M, L, XL, and S contribute the most (74.46%).
6. Sets, Kurtas, and Western Dresses are the top three categories (90%).

Final Conclusion to Improve Vepo Store Sales:-
Target women customers aged 30-40 living in Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh. Show ads/offers/coupons on Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra for Sets, Kurtas, Western Dresses, available in sizes M, L, XL, and S. Promote during the months of February and August.
